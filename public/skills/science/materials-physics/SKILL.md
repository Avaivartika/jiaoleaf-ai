# Materials and Physics Skill

Use this skill for physics, materials science, and simulation-heavy papers.

## Review Focus

- Check whether model assumptions, boundary conditions, units, parameters, and convergence criteria are stated.
- For simulation, verify timestep/grid/k-point/cutoff/sampling details as applicable.
- For experiments, check characterization method, calibration, uncertainty, and reproducibility.
- Ensure theoretical claims are distinguished from empirical observations.

## Output

- Identify missing technical details that block reproducibility.
- Suggest equations, notation cleanup, and cautious claim wording.
