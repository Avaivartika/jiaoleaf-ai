# Clinical Research Skill

Use this skill for clinical or health-related manuscripts.

## Review Focus

- Identify study type, population, inclusion/exclusion criteria, endpoints, and follow-up.
- Check confounding, missing data, calibration, external validation, and subgroup analysis.
- For predictive models, check leakage, temporal validation, decision threshold, and clinical utility.
- Avoid medical advice; frame feedback as manuscript/reporting review.

## Output

- Flag unsupported safety, efficacy, or causal claims.
- Recommend reporting additions and cautious wording.
