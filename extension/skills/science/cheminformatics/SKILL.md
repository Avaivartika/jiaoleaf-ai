# Cheminformatics Skill

Use this skill for chemistry, molecular machine learning, and computational drug discovery writing.

## Review Focus

- Check molecule split strategy: random, scaffold, temporal, or cluster split.
- Verify descriptor/fingerprint/model reporting and avoid train-test leakage.
- For docking or screening, check target preparation, protonation state, controls, and scoring limitations.
- For generation, check validity, uniqueness, novelty, diversity, synthesizability, and property constraints.

## Output

- Separate methodological risks from wording risks.
- Suggest precise revisions for claims, methods, and limitations.
